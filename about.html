About Blue Crew

The Team

The Blue Crew Team currently has 2 mentors: Richard Wilde and James Herlihy. Richard Wilde is a robotics teacher in the Foster Tech program at Mt. Blue Campus. James Herlihy is a former Spruce Mountain student and former member of team #3930 SMART.

The crew has many members and roles. Too many to list in fact, currently there is about 20 members.

Our Rookie Year

During our rookie year, our team acquired numerous awards, including Rookie All-Star. At our first competition, we won the first place award with our allies GUS and PVC Pirates.

Concussion Protocol

Our rookie bot, named "Concussion Protocol", has led us to victory many time and we still continue to run it for demos and public events. Concussion Protocol was named after our first competition with it. During most of our matches, the radio would "brown" out in the middle of a match after a hard impact. We finally found and fixed the issue, but thats why we named it "Concussion Protocol".
